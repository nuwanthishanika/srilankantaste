Chicken Rice

Ingredient

grain white rice - 1 kg
Boneless chicken -  200g
Chopped carrots - 1 cup
Chopped cabbage - 1 cup
Chopped leeks - 1 cup
Chopped onion - 1 cup
Eggs 3
Garlic 5og
Vegetable oil  
Black pepper powder - 1 tsp
Cumin powder - 1/2 tsp
Red chili - 1 tsp
Corn flour - 2 tbs
Salt  

  1. First wash the rice in cold water until the water runs clear.

  2. Add required amount of water and cook the rice.

  3. Cut the boneless chicken into bite-sized pieces and add them in a bowl.

  4. Add black pepper powder 1 tsp, cumin powder 1/2 tsp, red chili 1 tsp, corn flour 2 tbs to it.

  5. Mix well.

  6. Put some oil in the pan. After the oil heats up, add the boneless chicken. Fry well until brown.

  7. Then set aside it.

  8. Put some oil in the pan. After the oil heats up, add the garlic. Fry them till they become translucent.

  9. Then add the chopped vegetables. Carrots, leeks, cabbage, onions etc.

  10. Then add cracked eggs.

  11. Add some salt.

  12. After they are boiled, mix them with the boneless chicken and the rice and keep them in the pan for some time

  13. Garnish as desired and serve hot.
