Egg Rice

Ingredient

Grain white rice - 1kg
Chopped carrots - 1 cup
Chopped cabbage - 1 cup
Chopped leeks - 1 cup
Chopped onion - 1 cup

Vegetable oil
Eggs 5
Garlic 50g

Salt

  

Instructions

  1. First wash the rice in cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Add required amount of water and cook the rice.
  3. Put some oil in the pan. After the oil heats up, add the garlic. Fry them till they become translucent.
  4. Then add the chopped vegetables. Carrots, leeks, cabbage, onions etc.
  5. Then add cracked eggs.
  6. Add some salt to taste.
  7. After they are boiled, mix them with the rice and keep them in the pan for some time
  8. Garnish as desired and serve hot.
