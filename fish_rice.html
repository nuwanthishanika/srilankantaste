Fish Rice

Ingredient

Grain white rice - 1kg
White fish - 200g
Chopped carrots - 1cup
Chopped cabbage - 1cup
Chopped leeks - 1cup
Chopped onion - 1cup
Eggs 3

Vegetable oil

  

Garlic

- 50g

Salt

  

Black pepper powder

- 1 tsp

Cumin powder

- 1/2 tsp

Red chili

- 1 tsp

Corn flour

- 2 tbs

Instructions

  1. First wash the rice in cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Add required amount of water and cook the rice.
  3. Cut the fish fillets into bite-sized pieces and add them in a bowl.
  4. Add black pepper powder 1 tsp, cumin powder 1/2 tsp, red chili 1 tsp, corn flour 2 tbs to it.
  5. Mix well.
  6. Put some oil in the pan. After the oil heats up, add the fish fillets. Fry well until brown.
  7. Then set aside it.
  8. Put some oil in the pan. After the oil heats up, add the garlic. Fry them till they become translucent.
  9. Then add the chopped vegetables. Carrots, leeks, cabbage, onions etc.
  10. Then add cracked eggs.
  11. Add some salt to taste.
  12. After they are boiled, mix them with the fish fillets and the rice and keep them in the pan for some time
  13. Garnish as desired and serve hot.
