Egg Hopper

Ingredient

Rice flour - 500g
Coconut milk - 400ml
Fresh yeast - 15g
Oil for the pan  
Eggs  
Salt  
Some black paper powder  
Sugar - 1 tbsp

Instructions

Mix coconut milk, yeast and sugar together in a bowl and leave arounf 45min in na warm place.

•Sieve the flour and add into the mixture. Mix well until smooth batter forms. Cover the bowl using polethene and leave it around 4 hours until the dough doubles in size. After that, add salt and mix well.

•Keep hopper pan to heat. wipe it lightly with oil. Pour aroung 80ml of the mixture and swirl it around the pan, a small pool in the centre. crack an egg and pour it to the center of hopper.Add some black paper powder on it and cover it using the lid. Keep it around 2-4 minutes. When edges are golden color and the center is not liquidity, it is ready remove. Remove it carefully. You can tast it now.
