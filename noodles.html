Noodles

Ingredient

Instructions

Noodles 400g
Carrot 125 g
Leeks 100g
Bean 100g
Cabbage 100g
Onion 100g
2 or 3 eggs / 100g fried chicken  
Green chillies 20g
Ginger paste ½ tsp
Curry leaves 2 sprigs
Garlic paste 1 tsp
Turmeric powder 1/4 tsp
A piece of cinnamon  
Salt  
Black paper  
Coconut or vegetable oil  
  • Keep the noodles to boil.
  • While noodles are boil, wash and cut vegetables and onion into small pieces. If you add chicken, cut them also into small pieces.
  • After noodles are boiled, remove them from water and wash with cold water. Then spred on a flat surface.
  • Keep a pot to heat and add some oil in it.
  • When oil is heat, add garlic and ginger and keep to fry.
  • When smells good, add green chilies, onion and curry leaves.
  • After 10 minutes add carrot, leeks, beans, cabbage, onion and mix well.
  • After add turmeric powder, a piece of cinnamon, salt, black paper. Mix well.
  • After 5 minutes add noodles and remove from the heat after a while.
  • Serve warm.
